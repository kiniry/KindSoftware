The Kind Jiki

The Kind Jiki is a read/write web and is used to community discussion via knowledge representation and manipulation.

Behind the scenes, the Kind Jiki is supported by, of course, a Jiki server. Our Jiki server is a customized extension of that found at jiki.org in that our engine's data representation, transformation, and search are all realizations of Joseph Kiniry's Kind Theory.

Please visit the Kind Jiki and consider contributing!
