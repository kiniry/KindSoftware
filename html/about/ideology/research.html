Our Ideology

Research/Academic Ideology

Much of our work is grounded in recent academic research. We also perform quite a bit of in-house research as well.

KindSoftware encourages academic publications and participation from its participants, several of which are on program committees and are contributing editors of major conferences, journals, and books.

We believe in giving back to the academic community as well and sponsor professional organizations and conferences in the fields in which we work (e.g., formal methods, software engineering, reuse, distributed systems, etc.).
