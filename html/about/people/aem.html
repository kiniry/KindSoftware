Alan E. Morkan

He has a BA (Computer Science) International degree with French literature and language in University College Dublin, awarded in June 2005. He has been awarded a French Government Medal by the National University of Ireland.

His final year project in Computer Science entailed completing the KOA open-source internet voting system. His interests encompass software verification and trust and privacy in ubiquitous computing.

Alan was a Ph.D. student working on various topics related to the Mobius project and proof-carrying code for extended static checking, for which he earned an IRCSET fellowship. He is currently on leave.
