Chara A. Williams

Chara A. Williams received her Bachelors Degree in Biology from Williams College in 1996. She worked as a senior software developer for HealthShare Technology, Inc. in Acton, MA after graduation, where she devoted most of her spare time to graphic/web design for the company. She moved to California in late 1999 to pursue the artistic goals she has had since she could hold a pen and draw her brother as a 14 fingered stick figure in a batman costume. This pursuit has included co-founding and leading web development at DALi, Inc., independently designing web sites, and living in the Netherlands for a few years.

Chara designed the KindSoftware web site, which is tableless with validated CSS and XHTML. She also took all the photos displayed on the site.
