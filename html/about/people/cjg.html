Conor J. Gallagher

Conor Gallagher graduated in 2005, earning a Bachelors Degree in Computer Science at University College Dublin. Originally from Co. Wicklow, Ireland, he has resided primarily within the central Dublin area since 2001 with one year spent living and working in Canada in 2002-2003. His main interests in the field of Computer Science are the object orientated approach to software development with use of design patterns. His final year work focused on adding specification soundness checking to the extended static checker ESC/Java2. He is now a software engineer in industry while doing an MSc in Advanced Software Engineering at UCD.
